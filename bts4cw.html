Support – Frequently Asked Questions

What is your refund policy?

We offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase, provided that no services have been used. After 30 days, only partial refunds are available.

How do I change my account password?

Go to your account settings, click on “Security”, then “Change Password”. You will need to enter your current password and your new password twice.

Can I upgrade my plan at any time?

Yes — you can upgrade your plan at any time from the billing section of your dashboard. The upgrade takes effect immediately and you’ll be billed proportionally for the remainder of your billing cycle.
